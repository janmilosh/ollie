How I Got My New Home

(It Was Scary!)

This is how I arrived at my new home. Some idiot put me in this box, taped it up tight, then dumped me in the weeds. It was December, cold and wet.

I managed to claw my way out of the box and found a house nearby. You can't imagine my relief when the humans there let me stay.

I have a dog now. She's not overly excited about it, but she does like to partner-up to beg for food. I have humans too, but they're shy, so I didn't put up any of their pics.
